# Layout Patterns

Use this reference when the brief needs a concrete arrangement pattern.

## Poster Pattern

- Top: headline and visual hook.
- Middle: supporting detail or proof point.
- Bottom: date, location, CTA, or contact.

## Flyer Pattern

- Top: headline and subheadline.
- Middle: 2-4 information blocks or benefits.
- Bottom: contact, map cue, CTA, or sign-up detail.

## Social Post Pattern

- Visual lead first.
- One short headline or hook.
- One proof point or supporting line.
- CTA in the image or caption direction.

## Card News Pattern

- Card 1: hook or promise.
- Card 2-4: one idea per card.
- Final card: summary plus CTA.

## Banner Pattern

- Left or center: short headline.
- Secondary zone: one supporting phrase.
- Edge zone: CTA or brand cue.

## Thumbnail Pattern

- One dominant hook phrase.
- One focal subject or object.
- High-contrast emphasis on the first 2-4 words.
