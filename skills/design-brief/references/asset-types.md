# Asset Types

Use this reference when the user knows the channel or asset type but has not yet chosen the right message shape.

## Poster

- Best for: event promotion, announcements, one-message campaigns.
- Priority: headline, date or offer, one visual anchor, CTA.
- Avoid: long paragraphs and too many secondary details.

## Flyer

- Best for: local promotion, service overview, event details, simple handout.
- Priority: headline, supporting details, contact or location, CTA.
- Avoid: poster-style minimalism when the user actually needs practical details.

## Social Post

- Best for: single-message updates, short promotions, awareness content.
- Priority: hook, one proof point, CTA or next action.
- Avoid: dense layouts that assume long reading time.

## Card News

- Best for: multi-step explanation, listicle, before-and-after, mini education content.
- Priority: one message per card, short sequence, cumulative CTA on final card.
- Avoid: putting the whole story on the first card.

## Banner

- Best for: narrow spaces, simple campaign hooks, web promotion.
- Priority: short headline, brand or category cue, CTA.
- Avoid: more than one main message.

## Thumbnail

- Best for: click-driving preview image, video cover, content teaser.
- Priority: short hook, strong contrast, one visual focal point.
- Avoid: tiny text and generic wording.
