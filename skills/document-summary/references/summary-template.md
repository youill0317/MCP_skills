# Summary Template

Use this template to produce consistent, layered summaries.

## 1. Takeaway

> One sentence (max 30 words) capturing the most important conclusion.

## 2. Summary Paragraph

3-5 sentences covering:

- What the document is about
- The main findings or arguments
- The most significant implication or recommendation

## 3. Key Points

Bullet list of major claims, data, or decisions:

- Claim A
- Data point B
- Decision C

## 4. Open Questions / Unknowns

Items that are unclear, missing, or need verification:

- Unknown X: why it matters
- Gap Y: what additional data is needed

## 5. Section Breakdown (Detailed / Executive only)

For each major section of the source document:

### Section Title

- **Topic**: what this section covers
- **Key content**: main points from this section
- **Source reference**: file path and line range

## 6. Action Items (Executive only)

Numbered list of concrete next steps:

1. Action with owner and deadline context
2. Follow-up investigation or decision needed
