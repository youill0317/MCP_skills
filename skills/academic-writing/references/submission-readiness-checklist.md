# Submission Readiness Checklist

Use this checklist when preparing the final APA compliance report.

## Final Readiness Questions

1. Is the APA paper profile declared or reasonably inferred?
2. Are all required front-matter elements present or clearly marked missing?
3. Does the heading structure match the manuscript content?
4. Are all external claims cited or explicitly tagged?
5. Does the reference list map one-to-one with in-text citations?
6. Are quotations, tables, figures, footnotes, and appendices formatted and referenced properly?
7. Are unresolved formatting uncertainties listed as required fixes rather than hidden?

## Reporting Format

Return:

1. `Passed checks`
2. `Failed checks`
3. `Required fixes`
4. `Missing materials`
5. `Assumptions used`

## Fix Writing Style

1. Write fix requests as concrete actions.
2. Separate formatting fixes from evidence or metadata fixes.
3. Prefer exact missing-item language over vague warnings.
