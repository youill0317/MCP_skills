# Final Quality Gate

Run this checklist before returning final output.

## A. Structure Checks

1. Paper type is declared and section order matches the selected type.
2. Abstract and conclusion align with body claims.
3. Heading hierarchy is consistent and not skipped.

## B. Evidence and Citation Checks

1. Every external factual, numerical, or prior-work claim has support.
2. Unsupported external or prior-work claims are marked `[CITATION NEEDED]`.
3. Primary study results (for example, sample sizes or effect estimates) are traceable to analysis outputs.
4. In-text citations use APA 7 author-date format.
5. Direct quotes include page or paragraph locators.

## C. Reference List Checks

1. All in-text citations map to reference-list entries.
2. All reference-list entries are cited in text.
3. Entries are alphabetized and APA-normalized.
4. Missing fields are marked `[MISSING METADATA: field]`.

## D. Tables and Figures Checks

1. Numbering is sequential and unique.
2. Every table/figure is referenced in text.
3. Titles and notes are present and clear.

## E. Integrity Checks

1. No fabricated sources or metadata.
2. No invented statistics or findings.
3. Ambiguous source quality marked `[VERIFY SOURCE]`.

## Output of Quality Gate

Return:

1. `Passed checks`
2. `Failed checks`
3. `Required fixes`
4. `Assumptions used`
